Project Footprint

The Footprint humanitarian responder environmental impact calculator is a full-stack application that will give organizations the opportunity to upload their data and view a timeline-based breakdown of the environmental impacts. Project Footprint was a group project created by Blaze and Prime students Zack Stout, Jaffa Aharanov and Holly Tuhake

Technologies Used: Node.js, Express.js, Angular.js, Angular Material, PostgreSQL, Heroku, Chart.js, Passport.js
